Academic & Community

  • CompSoc - CompSoc is Scotland's largest tech society run by University of Edinburgh students.
  • Hoppers - The official society for Gender Minorities in Informatics at the University of Edinburgh.
  • InfBase - A student-run drop-in helpdesk for first and second year informatics students at the University of Edinburgh.
  • Programming Society - The official programming society. Aimed at beginners and those without prior opportunities to learn programming, offering LeetCode sessions and teaching of various programming languages.
  • Edinburgh Entrepreneurs - Do you have a business idea, or are you looking for networking opportunities? Check out Edinburgh Entrepreneurs.

There are many other societies and groups students can get involved in. If you don't see something you like, you can always create your own!
