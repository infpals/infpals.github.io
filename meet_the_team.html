Do you also want to become InfPALS Leader?

If you are in your 2nd or 3rd year, are passionate about the work we do as InfPALS Leaders and want to become part of the InfPALS family then becoming an official InfPALS Leader is for you!
InfPALS Leader is a voluntary position which we believe is very fulfilling, looks great on your resume, develops your leadership skill and helps you find like minded people. As a bonus every Leader gets their own InfPALS hoodie. This position takes about 3 hours per week. The applications usually open in April or May and they are sent to the university mails.
