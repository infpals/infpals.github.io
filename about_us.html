About Us

InfPALS is peer support for 1st year Informatics students.
As a group of 2nd and 3rd year students we will guide you through difficulties with course material and help you apply your knowledge in new situations to strengthen your understanding. Together we will ease your transition into your new-found university life!

What do we do

Throught discussing previous students' experiences we have devaloped student lead InfPALS sessions which are created to accomodate all of the students' educational and orientationl needs.

First semester we are primarly focusing on the content which is though in the course Introduction To Computation. We cover both parts of the corse which are computational logic as well as functional programming. The sessions cover previous week material or at the end of the semester exam topics.

Secend semester is usually spit into two parts. In the first half of the semester the sessions are foocused on the topics covered in Object Oriented Programming The second half of the semester is dedicated to covering topics like interview preparation, git and github and latex.

InfPALS Sessions

InfPALS sessions are happening weekly lasting about an hour.
These sessions usually start with an icebreaker then the main part of the sessions takes places. Main part of the session are activities/tasks which are reflecting topics covered in main computer science related degrees courses. After these activies/tasks students take a debreath, reflect on the session and freely ask any questions.
